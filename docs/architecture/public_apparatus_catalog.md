# Public apparatus catalog

The public apparatus catalog is the presentation authority for Cinelingus. The engineering filter registry remains the execution authority. Public naming must never alter stable filter IDs, contracts, recipes, cache keys, artifact schemas, or completed-run evidence.

The catalog models two independent dimensions:

- **Operating mode**: `solitary` (One Film) or `multiworld` (Several Films).
- **Discipline**: Chronomancy Engine, Contagion Laboratory, Memory Palace, Mask Workshop, Alchemical Engine, or Lexicon.

Multiworld is therefore not a discipline. Duplicate names such as Possession and Contagion are valid because operating mode disambiguates them.

## Status and invocation

- `available`: executable through a validated internal operator.
- `experimental`: executable, with provisional quality or behavior disclosed.
- `dormant`: cataloged future machinery with no execution action.
- `blocked`: contract work exists but a concrete dependency prevents execution.
- `hidden`: retained for compatibility and absent from the normal catalog.

Only available and experimental entries are invokable. Every current apparatus is `single_only`; no Procedure or chained execution is claimed.

## Capability tiers

| Tier | Minimum machinery |
|---|---|
| A | Dialogue selection, temporal restrictions, speaker restrictions, lexical proxies, and duration compatibility |
| B | Dialogue-audio gain, filtering, stretching, layering, ducking, repetition, or band limitation |
| C | Shot-aware montage and safe complete-moment assembly |
| D | Cross-film picture exchange or composition |
| E | Music, soundscape, scene-order, motif, or other cinematic-substance manipulation |
| F | Persistent Multiworld state and long-lived inter-film relationships |

## Truthfulness requirements

Lexicon entries disclose that their selection law is lexical rather than inferred emotion. Dream discloses its token-overlap and temporal-distance proxy. Dialect does not claim accent conversion. Dormant entries name their missing capability tier and cannot expose an invocation action.

Future apparatus concepts belong in design records until they have an executable law, capability declaration, parameters, failure behavior, provenance, acceptance criteria, determinism policy, and Procedure eligibility. A prose concept alone cannot become runnable.
